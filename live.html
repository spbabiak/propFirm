Forex

The Forex Market is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world with over $4 Trillion traded daily. Trading in the FX market involves taking a ‘position’ in one currency pair versus another in anticipation of that single currency outperfomring or strengthening against the second currency.


The spot Forex market is unique given that currencies trade in pairs (not in isolation).


Examples of ‘Major’ Forex pairs include EUR/USD, GBP/USD and USD/JPY. Exotic forex pairs include US Dollar versus the South African Rand and the US Dollar versus the Mexican Peso.


Forex pairs trade in ‘lots’. A standard lot is 100,000 units of currency. CFXM also offers Mini lots (10,000 units) and Micro lots (1,000 units).
