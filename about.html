About

Our Team

Apex Markets Group was founded by traders for traders. The markets move fast and participants need support from several different angles. Our diverse team of specialists aims to provide top notch service to every client around the clock. With clients all over the world, the Apex team is available 24 hours a day, 5 days to address your trading needs.


Our Mission

Our mission at Apex Markets Group is to provide traders with professional experience at every turn. With our same day deposit and withdrawal processing, clients are ensured to have the fastest access to their funds. The Apex team of market specialists will also keep you on top of the hottest market trends as they occur.
