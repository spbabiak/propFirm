Frequently Asked Questions

Which trading platforms does Apex Markets Group offer?

Apex Markets Group offers the award-winning Condor trading platform. Condor has TradingView charts directly embedded in the trading platform. Trade and track your orders directly from the charts so you’re always ahead of the game. For prop challenge participants, track your performance directly in the Contest Stats tab including profit target, maximum drawdown and duration of the challenge. Our brokerage also offers MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 via our liquidity partner Alchemy Markets. MetaTrader 4 allows traders to run Expert Advisors and trade instruments across a wide array of asset classes. MetaTrader 5 is also available upon request when opening an account.

How quickly are deposits and withdrawals processed?

Apex Markets Group is proud to process all deposits and withdrawals within 1 business day.

What is the minimum deposit?

The minimum deposit into the brokerage is $75 USD.

Does your broker offer bonuses?

Yes. Occasionally we do offer new account bonuses and keep our client updated on when such opportunities arise.

Can I trade cryptocurrencies with your broker?

Absolutely. We offer all the major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Rippe and more. We also offer more difficult to find Altcoins.

What this the duration of your prop challenges?

Each prop challenge has two phases. Each phase of the prop challenge lasts 30 days. Read more on our Prop Challenge page.

If I don’t pass my prop challenge on the first attempt, can I re-enter?

Yes. We encourage your resilience when attempting to pass the program. In fact on the 2nd attempt you can re-enter at 50% of the original fee.

What percentage payout do I receive if I pass your prop challenge?

Payout splits start at 80% for the first 60 days after passing and can increase to as high as 90%.

Can I trade with both the broker and prop firm at Apex Markets Group?

Absolutely. Traders can be members of both our brokerage and prop firm.

If I have a question or issue, how can I contact Apex Markets Group?

The best method of contact is by sending an email to info@apexmarketsgroup.com.
